What is MiC?

The Maths in Colors (MiC) is a dynamic online seminar series devoted to both pure and applied mathematics. The seminar aims to:

  • Bring together mathematicians to share and discuss their latest research.
  • Provide early-career researchers and PhD students with a supportive platform to present their work and receive constructive feedback.
  • Encourage collaboration and interdisciplinary exchange across the mathematical sciences.

Details

  • 🗓 Bi-weekly on Google Meet
  • ⏰ 40-min presentation + 10-min Q&A
  • 🌐 Open to researchers, faculty, doctoral candidates, and students
  • Visit MathsInColors on YouTube
  • Follow us on Facebook

The seminars will be recorded and posted on the Maths In Colors YouTube channel and Facebook page to ensure broad accessibility and allow everyone to watch or review the talks anytime. Join us to showcase your work, learn, and connect! Stay tuned for schedule and registration informations.
