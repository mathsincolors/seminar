What is MiC?

The Maths in Colors (MiC) is an online seminar series dedicated to pure and applied mathematics. The goals of the seminar are to:

  • Connect mathematicians to share their latest research
  • Support early-career researchers and PhD students with a platform to present and get feedback
  • Foster collaboration and interdisciplinary exchange

Details

  • 🗓 Bi-weekly on Google Meet
  • ⏰ 40-min presentation + 10-min Q&A
  • 🌐 Open to researchers, faculty, doctoral candidates, and students

The seminars will be recorded and posted on the Maths In Colors YouTube channel and Facebook page to ensure broad accessibility and allow everyone to watch or review the talks anytime Join us to showcase your work, learn, and connect! Stay tuned for schedule and registration info. https://www.youtube.com/@MathsInColors Follow us on Facebook
